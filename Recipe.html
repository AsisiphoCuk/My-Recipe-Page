GREEN SALAD

üïû 15 minutes    üç¥ 3 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 5 Cups lettuce
  • 1 Cucumber
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan shreds
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 Avocado
  • 1/2 cup ripe green olives
  • 1/4 tspn pepper flakes
  • Homemade italian dressing

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Make the Homemade italian dressing.
  2. Wash your ingredients
  3. Chop the lettuce.
  4. Thinly slice the red onion.
  5. Slice tomatoes and olives in half.
  6. Peel and chop the cucumber and avocado.
  7. Mix together all the ingredients in a bowl.
  8. Toss to combine
  9. Add the salad dressing and Enjoy!üòã
NOTES
If you are making the salad in advance, refrigerate the components separately.
Bring the dressing to room temperature before serving.
